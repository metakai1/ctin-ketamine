## About These Sources

These 16 peer-reviewed publications represent foundational research in:

- **Neuroscience**: Mechanisms of ketamine action (Li et al., 2010; Zanos & Gould, 2018)
- **Clinical Psychiatry**: Treatment efficacy in depression and PTSD (Berman et al., 2000; Feder et al., 2014; Zarate et al., 2006)
- **Neuroplasticity**: Synaptic dysfunction and rapid antidepressant effects (Duman & Aghajanian, 2012; Duman et al., 2019)
- **Psychopharmacology**: Dissociation and therapeutic processes (Luckenbaugh et al., 2014; Morgan & Curran, 2012)

All sources are publicly accessible through academic databases and institutional libraries.
